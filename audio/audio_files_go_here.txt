FILENAMING SCHEME:

equalizationstandard.title.format

Where 'equalizaionstandard' can be one of the following:

Professional:
"IEC1_15": IEC 1 at 15 in/s;
"IEC1_7": IEC 1 at 7.5 in/s;
"IEC2_15": IEC 2 at 15 in/s;
"IEC2_7": IEC 2 at 7.5 in/s;

Domestic:
"NAB_15": NAB at 15 in/s;
"NAB_7": NAB at 7.5 in/s;
"NAB_3": NAB at 3.75 in/s.

Example:
IEC1_15.Frederic Chopin - Fantaisie Impromptu.mp3

EQ standard: IEC 1 at 15 in/s;
Track title: Frederic Chopin - Fantaisie Impromptu;
Filetype: mp3.
